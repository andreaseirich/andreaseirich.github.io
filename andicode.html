Case Study

Problem

As a freelance web developer, I needed a professional online presence that:

  • Clearly communicates my service offerings (websites, web apps, APIs, maintenance)
  • Showcases past projects and the technologies I use
  • Provides an easy way for potential clients to get in touch
  • Reflects modern design standards and technical competence
  • Stands out visually while remaining fast and accessible

Solution

Designed and built a visually striking single-page website with:

  • Dark Theme: Professional dark design with blue accent color and animated background orbs
  • Glassmorphism UI: Modern navigation and card elements with backdrop blur effects
  • Interactive Elements: 3D card tilt effects on hover, scroll-reveal animations, and smooth transitions
  • Clear Structure: Dedicated sections for services, projects, technologies, about, and contact

My Responsibilities

  • Design: Complete UI/UX design with dark theme, glassmorphism, and custom animations
  • Development: Full frontend implementation with HTML5, TailwindCSS, and JavaScript
  • Content: Service descriptions, project showcases, and technology listings
  • Contact Integration: Functional contact form with validation
  • Deployment: Custom domain setup and hosting configuration for andicode.de

Status

Live & Maintained – The website is live at andicode.de and actively maintained.
